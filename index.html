💡 B2 vs C1 — Concrete Differences

Same idea — different level

B2 (what you say now) C1 (what you'll say)
"I think it's important" "I'd argue this is rather pivotal"
"okay okay okay" "noted" / "duly noted" / "point taken"
"If we do this, it will be better" "Were we to pursue this, the outcome would be markedly stronger"
"I don't agree" "I'd respectfully push back on that — the data suggests..."
"This is a big problem" "This poses a significant risk to our delivery timeline"
"We need to decide" "A decision is overdue — I'd propose we align on this today"
