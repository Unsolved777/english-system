🧠 Neurolearning Hacks

💤 Sleep consolidation

Review Anki cards right before sleep — the brain replays and consolidates what was last reviewed. This is why your company course worked better. Anki's spaced repetition schedules exactly the right cards.

😮 Emotion-linked vocabulary

Cards from your own meetings are remembered better than generic examples. "To pose a significant risk" — you remember the meeting where you should have said this. Emotion = stronger memory trace.

🔁 Spaced repetition (automated)

Anki handles this automatically. You just review what it shows you. 10 min/day beats 2 hours once a week by a large margin. The fill-in-the-blank format adds active recall on top.

📖 Input from real reading

Every Kindle book you read is now a learning source. Aqua = Anki. You're not adding study time — you're extracting value from time you were already spending on reading.
